# Executive Summary

This report presents the results of a technology risk assessment performed on the organisation’s technology environment. The assessment focuses on identifying risks affecting system confidentiality, integrity, availability, and operational resilience.

The organisation relies on a combination of endpoint devices, internal systems, cloud platforms, and network infrastructure to support daily operations. While baseline controls may be present, gaps in governance, monitoring, and process standardisation can increase exposure to operational disruption and security incidents.

The objective of this assessment is to:

- Identify key technology risks affecting the organisation
- Evaluate the effectiveness of existing controls
- Assess the likelihood and impact of identified risks
- Provide practical recommendations for risk mitigation

The assessment identified several technology risks commonly faced by small-to-medium enterprises, particularly in the areas of:

- Access governance
- Patch and vulnerability management
- Asset visibility
- Backup and recovery assurance
- Incident response maturity
- Third-party and vendor dependency

Addressing these risks will improve operational resilience, strengthen governance practices, and reduce exposure to technology-related disruptions.
