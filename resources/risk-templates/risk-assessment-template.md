# Technology Risk Assessment Template

## 1. Executive Summary
Provide a high-level overview of the organisation, the purpose of the assessment, and the key risk themes identified.

---

## 2. Assessment Scope
Define the systems, processes, and technology environments included in the review.

Typical areas may include:

- Endpoint security
- Access management
- Patch management
- Network security
- Backup and recovery
- Incident response
- Vendor dependencies

---

## 3. Methodology
Describe how the assessment was conducted, including risk identification, analysis, and evaluation.

---

## 4. Technology Environment Overview
Provide a description of the technology environment being assessed, including systems, devices, networks, and cloud services.

---

## 5. Risk Identification
List the key risks identified during the assessment.

---

## 6. Risk Analysis
Evaluate each risk based on likelihood and impact.

---

## 7. Findings
Provide detailed descriptions of each identified risk and the associated control weaknesses.

---

## 8. Overall Risk Themes
Summarise the most significant risk trends across the organisation.

---

## 9. Conclusion
Provide a high-level conclusion on the organisation’s technology risk posture.

Detailed remediation actions should be documented separately in the recommendations register.
